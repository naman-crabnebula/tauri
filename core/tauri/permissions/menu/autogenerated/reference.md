- `allow-new`
- `allow-append`
- `allow-prepend`
- `allow-insert`
- `allow-remove`
- `allow-remove-at`
- `allow-items`
- `allow-get`
- `allow-popup`
- `allow-create-default`
- `allow-set-as-app-menu`
- `allow-set-as-window-menu`
- `allow-text`
- `allow-set-text`
- `allow-is-enabled`
- `allow-set-enabled`
- `allow-set-accelerator`
- `allow-set-as-windows-menu-for-nsapp`
- `allow-set-as-help-menu-for-nsapp`
- `allow-is-checked`
- `allow-set-checked`
- `allow-set-icon`
